Kode-E Business Model Canvas

Problems
  • During these challenging times, many NYC rsidents are grappling with the socio-economical effects of COVID-19.
  • Many are unaware of grants, low cost health care, free vaccinations, childcare, remote employment, and food pantries in their area.
    • Consequentially, many out-of-work individuals, including the elderly are left with limited essential resources.
Solution
  • Kode-E's software can connect NYC residents with economic, health, and social ervice resources.
  • Kode-E will use the user's locale to bring up a list of nearby free/low-cost resources. This will be done in a map/itemized list similar to the Google Maps' interface.
  • Kode-E will also perform as a personal assistant by setting alarms, mapping out useful features such as goal planning, online shopping assistance(creating shopping checklist, wallet function, EBT balance tracker, coupon locater, etc),and a host of other useful customizable features.
Key Metrics
  • The trackable traffic and the amount of daily interactions with Kode-E will indicate whether or not the app is doing well.
  • Ad revenue earned from users interacting with Kode-E can be tracked by Kode-E's development team.
Unique Value Propositions
  • Kode-E is designed specifically for low-moderate income NYC residents and will offer specialized events for its users to help reduce stresses that come along with limited finacial resources. Kode-E offers an unique humanistic approach to assisting the user.
  • People are looking for support, a personable avatar such as Kode-E can bring users comfort and reassurance. This is a feature Google Assistant, Siri, and Alexa do not offer.
  • Kode-E is a free of charge Community Assitant app. Kode-E can also offer intutive emotional support. He's more of a friend or buddy; not just a talking search engine/lackluster assistant
Unfair Advantage
  • Kode-E compiles all resources in one place. Users do not have to spend hours on Google searching for the assistance the require.
  • A lot of the information for Kode-E is located online. Devs will work closely with major companies to bring exclusive information to Kode-E
  • Kode-E's voice is also unique and one of a kind. CodeByUfu's founder, Ufuoma G, will be voicing Kode-E.
Channels
  • Kode-E will be available for smart devices and PC/Apple computers/laptops.
  • The Kode-E app can be downloaded free of charge.
  • An optional monthly subscription with plenty perks, can be available in the near future
Customer Segments
  • My target audience will be low income residents who are looking for a way up and out of poverty
  • My ideal user traits will be curious, humble, hardworking and tenacious.
  • Kode-E will offer a small daily stipend of $0.05 - $.0.20 to users who complete simple reviews and surveys about community resources they engagened with via Kode-E.
Cost Structure
  • It may cost a total of $10,000-$50,000 to create Kode-E
  • Maintenance of Kode-e will cost significantly less.
Revenue Streams
  • Kode-E will generate revenue from the ads found in the load up screen. Users will have to engage with the ads for a few seconds in order to proceed.
  • Kode-will eventually team up larger companies for sponsorship.
  • Kode-E at some point offer a monthly subscription to users. The monthly fee will come with substantial perks
